## What Flow State Studio Does

Flow State Studio acts like a content operating space for people who want a calmer path from rough ideas to finished writing. Planning, drafting, and AI-assisted generation live together in one workspace, helping content stay coherent without the friction of bouncing between separate tools.

The workflow is intentionally simple and supportive for neurodivergent creatives who do better with less clutter and fewer interruptions. Rather than splitting outlines, notes, and drafts across multiple apps, you can keep everything inside one flow and work through it in the order that suits you.

## Usage

Begin with an outline, then move into drafting and AI-assisted generation whenever it helps.

Typical workflow:
1. Open Flow State Studio in your browser.
2. Create or load a content plan.
3. Draft your writing inside the workspace.
4. Use AI generation when you want support with ideas or text expansion.
5. Revisit the plan and refine the final piece as you go.

Because the app is built around one continuous content flow, it suits short notes, evolving drafts, and structured planning work.
